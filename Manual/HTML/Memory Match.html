On the Subject of Memory Match

Flip. Flip. Aw, dang it!

  • Upon activation, the module deals six pairs of cards face down on the table.
  • Selecting a card flips it over. If a selected pair matches, both cards remains face up, otherwise both will flip back over.
  • Flip all cards face up before time runs out.
